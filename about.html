About Me

Team-oriented, analytical, and timely quality assurance analyst with a commitment to ongoing personal growth.

Hi, my name is Mariam and I work as a Quality Assurance analyst both remotely and full time. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering.

At the end of 2021, I didn't know where my life was going. After some serious self-reflection about my passions and interests, I committed to learning everything I could about data analytics, and the tech industry.

My journey to software quality assurance has been paved by my curiosity and technical proficiency, which have also aided me in investigating software development errors and prevent defects in software production.

I am available for short-term projects and part-time longer-term engagements working with data to make effective use of their data through data preparation, analysis and visualization, all the way to impactful presentation of insights.
